Cyber Security, Risk and Resilience

Alex studied Computer Security at undergraduate level and graduated with a First Class BSc. Postgraduate work in Cyber Security, Risk and Resilience builds on that foundation with a deeper focus on how organisations cope with real pressure — where people miscommunicate, controls drift, systems fail at the wrong moment and someone has to make a decision before the damage spreads.

The approach is simple: look at people, technology and process as a system. Understand what is supposed to happen, then look at what actually happens. The gap between those two things is where most risk lives.
