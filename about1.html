About

By Alex • Published: 22 January 2026 • Updated: 22 January 2026

CyberAlex

Cyber-first, practical, systems-minded.

I’m Alex — a cyber security practitioner with a First Class BSc (Hons) in Computer Security, focused on translating complex technical risk into decisions that stand up in the real world.

CyberAlex is both a portfolio and a living knowledge base: tools, write-ups, frameworks, and notes that prioritise clarity, evidence, and usefulness. You’ll also see a small amount of non-cyber material (advanced driving, photography, collections) — included because it reflects the same underlying habits: discipline, precision, and continuous improvement.
