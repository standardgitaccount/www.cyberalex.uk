Compound Interest Forecast Calculator

This calculator helps estimate the future value of your investments or savings using compound interest, with or without annual contributions.

Results will appear here.

About This Tool

This general-purpose compound interest calculator helps you forecast long-term financial growth. Whether saving for retirement, investing in the stock market, or growing a cash reserve, it shows the impact of consistent contributions and compound returns over time.

How It Works

Each year, your investment grows by the interest rate you specify. Any annual contributions are added at the start of the year before growth is applied. The total is recalculated yearly using compound interest.

Formula Used

Total = Initial × (1 + r)^t + Annual × [((1 + r)^t - 1) / r]

Where:

  • r = annual interest rate (decimal)
  • t = number of years
  • Annual = annual top-up contribution
