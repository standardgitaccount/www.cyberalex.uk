Encryption Tool

By Alex • Published: 29 November 2025 • Updated: 29 November 2025

Encrypt and decrypt text directly in your browser using AES, with all processing performed locally to keep your data private, secure, and fully offline.

This tool is designed for quick experiments, demonstrations, and small snippets of data. It is not a replacement for formally designed cryptographic systems in production environments.

At a Glance

  • Runs entirely in your browser – no data is sent to a server.
  • Uses AES in a modern browser-based implementation.
  • Useful for testing, demonstrations and learning.

Note: Do not paste highly sensitive data unless you fully trust the device and environment you are using.
