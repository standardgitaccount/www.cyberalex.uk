What this could mean

Under current UK rules, you can usually take up to 25% of your pension tax-free (subject to the Lump Sum Allowance), with the remainder taxed as income when withdrawn. The figures above do not guarantee any level of income and are not a substitute for regulated financial advice.

Why this tool is different

  • Uses date of birth to remove age-rounding errors.
  • Separates earliest access age from State Pension age.
  • Models net vs gross contributions explicitly.
  • Shows low / mid / high outcomes instead of false precision.
  • Exposes all assumptions via a full audit trail.

Limitations & disclaimer

This tool is for educational purposes only. It does not account for changes in legislation, investment selection, platform fees, inflation, health, career breaks, or future tax policy. Pension rules and personal circumstances can and do change.

This is not regulated financial advice. If you are unsure, consider speaking to a regulated financial adviser or using official guidance services such as Pension Wise.
