Recommended Browser Extensions in 2025

Introduction

Recommended Browser Extensions and Add-Ons for Privacy, Security, and Convenience in 2025.

There are millions of browser extensions available, but how do you find the ones that can benefit you? This page will list my recommendations for 2025. Note that some applications listed may be available from more stores than just the Chrome Web Store and the Firefox add-on site.

Extensions

Essentials

  • uBlock Origin for > Chrome or > Firefox.
  • I still don't care about cookies for > Chrome or > Firefox.
  • Privacy badger for > Chrome or > Firefox.
  • Cookie autodelete for > Chrome or > Firefox.
  • DuckDuckGo Privacy Essentials for > Chrome or > Firefox.

For your Passwords

  • KeePassXC (Free but Requires Reading) for > Chrome or > Firefox.
  • LastPass (Has free version) for > Chrome or > Firefox.
  • Nordpass (Has free version) for > Chrome or > Firefox.
  • Dashlane (Paid) for > Chrome or > Firefox.

Convienience on YouTube

  • YouTube Sponsor Skip for > Chrome or > Firefox.
  • YouTube Return Dislike for > Chrome or > Firefox.
  • YouTube AutoHD for > Chrome or > Firefox.
  • YouTube Nonstop for > Chrome or > Firefox.
