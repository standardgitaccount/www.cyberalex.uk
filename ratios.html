Liquid Mixing Ratio Calculator

This tool helps you calculate the volumes and percentages of up to three chemicals to make a desired total volume of liquid. It's ideal for chemistry labs, DIY projects, or hobbyists.

Results will appear here.

About This Tool

This tool allows users to input a total target volume and individual volumes of up to three chemicals. When one or two values are provided, the remaining field(s) will autofill to meet the target volume if possible. It then calculates the percentage of each chemical in the final mix.

Common Uses

  • Laboratory liquid mixture calculations
  • Homebrew or DIY chemical recipes
  • Paint, resin, and cleaning product formulation

Instructions

  1. Enter the total desired volume in millilitres (ml)
  2. Input the volume of up to three components
  3. Leave one or more components blank to autofill
  4. Click Calculate Ratios to view percentage breakdown

Disclaimer

This tool is for educational and convenience purposes only. It assumes all chemicals are compatible and miscible. Always consult a material safety data sheet (MSDS) and a qualified professional when working with hazardous substances.

Privacy

All calculations are performed locally in your browser. No data is stored or transmitted.
