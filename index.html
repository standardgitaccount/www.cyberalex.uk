Professional Profile

What I Work On

I focus on how organisations manage security, risk and resilience in real conditions, not idealised ones. My work sits across systems, people and process, with a strong emphasis on:

  • Turning frameworks into practical workflows and tools.
  • Analysing risk in realistic, business-driven scenarios.
  • Improving repeatability through automation and clear documentation.

How I Tend to Work

I like to work end-to-end — understanding a system, questioning its assumptions, and iterating until it makes sense. In reality, this looks like:

  • Breaking messy problems down into their moving parts.
  • Exploring, testing, refining, and simplifying.
  • Avoiding blame but communicating limitations honestly.
  • Taking feedback seriously and adjusting quickly.

What I’m Looking For

I’m looking for early-career roles that blend technical security work with risk, governance or resilience. Long-term, I’m aiming for security architecture or leadership pathways where:

  • Trade-offs and constraints are taken seriously.
  • Communication is as important as tooling.
  • Good reasoning is valued over buzzwords.
